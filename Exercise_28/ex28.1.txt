Ruby Assignement and Comparions Operators 

==        equals 
!=        not equals
<         smaller than 
>         bigger than
<=        smaller than or equals 
>=        bigger than or equals
===       equal (case statements)
<=        compare 
.eql?     is it equal (same type and equal values)
.equal?   is it equal (same object id)
+=        add equals 
-=        subtract equals 
*=        mulitply equals 
/         divide equals 
%=        modulus equals 
**=       exponent equals
